Arcadia High School

Coding Club


Four officers, Patrick Liu, Sandra Tang, Stella Saw and Samuel Berkun, volunteered at Field Elementary School in Pasadena. They taught kids how to code through Hour of Code, Scratch, and HTML. Students also experienced playing Minecraft with a keyboard and monitor hooked up to a Raspberry Pi.

Overview
Coding Club aims to educate students at Arcadia High School about coding languages and to unite those with common interests. Meetings are held in C108 at Lunch in Mr. Villalobos's room. Members
Provide feedback and suggestions in our Google Form.
